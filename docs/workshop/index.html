AgentSociety Challenge Workshop

FOREWORD

The AgentSociety Challenge Workshop brings together academic researchers, industry practitioners, and competition participants to explore innovative applications of large language model (LLM) agents in web experiences. Following the successful completion of the AgentSociety Challenge, this workshop will showcase the winning solutions, provide insights into the latest research directions, and foster discussions on the future of LLM agents in online review platforms and recommendation systems.

The workshop will feature presentations from the top-performing teams from both the User Modeling Track and the Recommendation Track. These presentations will highlight novel approaches, technical innovations, and key insights gained through the competition. Additionally, invited speakers from academia and industry will share their perspectives on the broader implications of agent-based systems for the web.

This workshop serves as a unique platform for knowledge exchange, networking, and collaboration among participants interested in advancing the state-of-the-art in LLM agent technologies. Join us to be part of the exciting discussion on how LLM agents are reshaping our interaction with online platforms and opening new avenues for research and innovation.
